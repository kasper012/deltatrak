ColdTrak Data Central is a cloud-based service that helps our customers retrieve, view, analyze and share critical temperature data during transportation, or stationary storage and displaying points, with their peers and partners throughout the world, 24/7. The service is available on a monthly subscription basis that covers up to 5 user sites with the option to add additional sites.

Temperature information is uploaded using DeltaTrak’s ColdTrak Manager or ColdTrak Tag-It software. The ColdTrak Data Central service enables viewing of trip data and graphs just minutes after the data loggers are downloaded at the receiving facilities. This allows receivers to take immediate action if there is evidence of out-of-range temperature conditions. Advanced search and multi-graph view features help identify frequent temperature abuse patterns and improve the decision making process at the operational level.
