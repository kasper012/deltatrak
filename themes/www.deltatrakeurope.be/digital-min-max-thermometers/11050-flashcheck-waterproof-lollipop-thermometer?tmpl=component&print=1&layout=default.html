Applications: Food Establishments, Distribution Centers, Cold Storage, Processing Facilities

The FlashCheck Waterproof Lollipop Thermometer (Model 11050) is ideal for food establishments and distribution centers. With the thermometer’s auto-calibration and min/max features, the small-diameter probe is ideal for measuring temperature of "thin mass" food such as meat patties and chicken/fish fillets.
