Applications: Transport of Produce, Prepared Foods, Pharmaceuticals, Chemicals, Storage Units, Refrigerators

The FlashLink® PDF VU In-Transit Logger has on-board software that automatically creates PDF and CSV trip reports as soon as the unit is plugged into a PC. No cradles, reading devices or adapters are needed. The secure, encrypted PDF report can be saved on the local hard drive, making it convenient to email and archive for quality assurance, traceability, and GDP. Service is available to configure customized logging parameters, such as sample intervals and high/low alarm settings.

This single-use in-transit temperature logger is an ideal cold chain management solution for monitoring temperature sensitive products as they are transported through the supply chain.

A built-in USB connector and on-board software make it convenient for global distribution, since special reading devices are not required and there is no need to purchase software for downloading trip data. It is particularly important to have these features when loggers accompany products to remote locations; it means that receivers will have quick and easy access to temperature reports as soon as a shipment arrives.

Packaging

Each FlashLink® PDF VU In-Transit Logger is mounted on a bright green shipping card, making it easy to find when a load arrives. The card has an area for documenting shipping information and features a peel-away barcode label that can be attached to the bill of lading or other shipping documents, for traceability. It also provides shipper instructions in English and Spanish.

Users can scan the QR code to quickly access more information from a URL, including the product specifictions.
