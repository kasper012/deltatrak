Applications: Temperature screening to detect fevers in children and adults

Forehead infrared temperature thermometers ensure accurate and stable body temperature readings.

From a distance of 3 cm - 5 cm (1.2 in - 2 in), simply point the device at forehead and read temperature shown on LCD screen.
