Applications: Ideal for extremely hot areas, moving objects, hard to reach places

This fast, accurate thermometer is ideal for use anywhere food is processed, prepared, handled, served, packed, stored, shipped and received. The sharp tip thermocouple probe measures internal temperature, while the infrared sensor allows users to take instant surface temperature without touching, contaminating or causing damage. It is also ideal for measuring extremely hot areas, moving objects and hard to reach places. The unique multi-beam laser sighting ensures accurate aim at the target, a green backlight indicates when temperature is within the HACCP Food Safety Zone and a red backlight indicates measurements are in the Temperature Danger Zone where foodborne pathogens thrive and multiply.
