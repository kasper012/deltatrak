Thermal Mapping

Internal temperatures of storage facilities vary, meaning thermal mapping is vital for meeting FDA requirements.

Temperature fluctuations

Thermal mapping, or facility temperature profiling, is legally required by the FDA for facilities storing and distributing certified cold chain drug related products.

Cutting corners

Infrequent, occasional temperature checks are all too common among facility operators, leading to limited record keeping and a lack of validated documentation.

Year-round control

DeltaTrak’s temperature monitoring solutions enable facilities to carry out thorough thermal mapping covering winter, summer and “ambient” conditions, while providing full audit-compliant documentation.
