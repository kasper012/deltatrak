Model 22144

Order
  • Two-channel logging capability

  • Glycol buffered probe to emulate condition of vaccines and most fluids
  • Uninterrupted data transmission during power outages
  • 30-hour back-up battery
  • Secure PDF reports, analytics and queries
  • Audit trail to document regulatory compliance
  • 21 CFR Part 11 compliant cloud-based administration system

This wireless system is ideal for refrigerator and freezer units where vaccines, pharmaceuticals or food products are stored. It continuously monitors time and temperature around the clock, using Wi-Fi and cellular communication to transmit data to a cloud-based application where it is visible in real-time and can be accessed 24/7. This secure web application allows users to remotely configure the WRM unit with logging parameters such as sample interval, high/low alarm limits, as well as alerts and notifications to designated personnel who can take immediate corrective action when excursions occur.

FlashTrak WRM ensures data is never lost even in the event of a power failure. This feature is essential in order to maintain the quality, efficacy and safety of products. The WRM is a valuable tool for documenting regulatory compliance, for maintaining continuity of business and following disaster recovery plans after a power outage.
